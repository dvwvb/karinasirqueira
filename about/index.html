I’m Karina Sirqueira, a Brazilian designer at Work & Co in Brooklyn, NY.

I was lucky to start my career at globo.com, where I got passionate about designing digital products. After 5 years I moved to the agency world. First Huge in Rio. Then Elephant in SF. Now Work & Co, where I’ve worked with great clients like Apple, MTA, Aldo, and Clover. I’ve also been developing new skills and challenging myself to come up with a new idea every day that combines design and code.

As a female designer, I’ve needed to overcome some barriers in my career. It’s taught me to push through and stand up for myself. I want to inspire other designers to do the same, so don’t hesitate to reach out. I’m always online.
